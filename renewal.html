Arkansas Concealed Handgun License Renewal

Step One: Complete the Online Renewal Form on the Arkansas State Police Website

Step Two: Contact Us and schedule your in-person renewal class (the class takes 30 mins or less)

Step Three: Mail the completed form we provide you to the Arkansas State Police Headquarters

Step Four: You will then recieve your new license in the mail. It will be valid for five (5) years.

We are available to provide renewal classes during most weekends and Mondays.
